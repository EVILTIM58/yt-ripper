About yt-ripper

yt-ripper is a small, self-hosted video download utility. Paste a video URL, pick a quality, and the file is saved to the server's storage. It runs as a single lightweight container with no accounts, no tracking, and no ads.

What it's for

This tool exists for legitimate personal archiving: saving copies of your own uploads, downloading Creative Commons and public-domain material, and keeping offline copies of content you have permission to retain. It is not intended for redistributing other people's work, and the Terms of Use require every user to respect copyright law and the terms of the platforms they download from.

Who runs it

yt-ripper is privately operated from Alturas, California, USA. It is a personal project, not a commercial service.
