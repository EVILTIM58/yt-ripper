Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy describes what yt-ripper actually collects — which is very little, because the site has no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

What we collect

When you submit a download, the server temporarily holds the video URL you pasted and the progress of that job in memory; this is discarded when the server restarts. The web server does not write access logs. Downloaded files are stored on the operator's private server storage.

Cookies and local storage

The site itself sets no cookies and uses no browser local storage. If the site is protected by Cloudflare Access, Cloudflare sets authentication cookies to keep you signed in — see Cloudflare's privacy policy for how those are handled.

Third parties

Traffic to this site passes through Cloudflare (our network provider). Download requests are made from the server to the source video platform; that platform sees the server's connection, not yours. We do not sell, share, or transmit any visitor data to anyone.

Advertising

This site currently serves no advertising. If third-party advertising (such as Google AdSense) were ever added, this policy would be updated first to disclose it, including third-party vendors' use of advertising cookies to serve ads based on prior visits, and a link to Google Ads Settings for opting out of personalized advertising.

Your rights

Since we hold essentially no personal data about visitors, there is usually nothing to request — but you can contact the operator with any privacy question.

This policy is a template written for this specific application and is not legal advice. Have an attorney review it before relying on it for a commercial service.
